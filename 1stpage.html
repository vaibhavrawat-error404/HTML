ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

    Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to think, learn, a
    nd solve problems like humans, often performing tasks that normally require
    human intelligence such as recognizing speech, making decisions, or playing
    games.  Unlike traditional software that follows explicit instructions, AI 
    systems learn from data, identify patterns, and improve their performance over
    time without constant human guidance.

KEY CHARACTERSTICS:

    1.Automation: It automates repetitive tasks, increasing efficiency in
                industries like healthcare, finance, and transportation. 
    2.Decision Making: AI analyzes large datasets to make precise
                predictions, such as diagnosing diseases or detecting fraud. 
    3.Adaptability: The technology evolves by learning from new data,
                allowing it to handle complex problems like language translation 
                and image recognition. 
    4.Limitations: Current AI lacks true consciousness or mindfulness 
                and operates within the constraints of its training data.
